About buy-a-brainrot

Fast, secure, and personal delivery — built for players who value trust.

Hi — I’m the creator of buy-a-brainrot

I started this project to make item delivery simple, fast, and completely safe. Every trade is handled directly by me — no bots, no middlemen, and no unnecessary steps. I’ll personally add you on samsalt294 after checkout to complete your delivery in a private server.

Delivery times are quick: 5–20 minutes for in-stock items and up to 60 minutes during peak hours. If there’s ever a delay, you’ll always be updated.

I built buy-a-brainrot around one idea — trust through direct trade. No passwords, no risks, just clean, fast, verified delivery.
